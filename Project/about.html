Our Story

The Indy Food Truck Rally began in the summer of 2020 with a simple mission: to connect the city's hungry residents with the best mobile chefs in Indiana. What started as a small gathering of three trucks in a parking lot has grown into a weekly city-wide event.

Community Mission

We believe in supporting small businesses. Every truck at our rally is locally owned and operated. By visiting the rally, you aren't just getting a great mealâ€”you are helping a local family achieve their culinary dreams.

Safety First

All vendors participating in the Indy Food Truck Rally are fully licensed and undergo rigorous health inspections. We maintain strict cleanliness standards to ensure a safe and delicious environment for everyone.
