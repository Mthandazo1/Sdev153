Welcome to the Rally

Welcome to Indianapolis's premier weekly food event! The Indy Food Truck Rally brings together the best local chefs, bakers, and mobile kitchens in one convenient location.

Whether you are looking for spicy street tacos, gourmet burgers, or a sweet dessert to finish the night, we have something for everyone. Our lineup changes weekly, so there is always something new to taste.

This Week's Highlights

  • Live Music: Local bands playing every Friday night.
  • Family Friendly: Games and seating areas for kids.
  • Pet Friendly: Bring your furry friends (on a leash)!
