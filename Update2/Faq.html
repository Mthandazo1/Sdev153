Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. Here are the most common things people ask us.

Do the trucks accept credit cards?
Yes! almost all of our vendors accept Visa, Mastercard, and Apple Pay. Some also accept cash.

Are dogs allowed at the rally?
Absolutely. We are a pet-friendly event, but we ask that all dogs remain on a leash at all times.

Is there seating available?
We provide a limited number of picnic tables. We encourage guests to bring their own folding chairs or picnic blankets.

What if it rains?
The rally is a rain-or-shine event! If there is severe weather (lightning/thunder), we may pause operations for safety.
